4. Bidding Strategy*
i
How do bidding strategies work?

Choose how the system adjusts your bids in real time to help you win auctions efficiently.

Select your bidding strategy to manage how much you spend per click.

Dynamic Bid (Up & Down) Best for growth
Recommended
We bid up to 2x for top placements and scale back during low competition to ensure your budget goes where it counts.
Example: With a AED 1.00 bid, we may raise it to AED 2.00 for high-value placements or lower it to the minimum.
Dynamic Bid (Down Only) Best for budget control
We lower your bids when competition is low to help you spend your budget more efficiently.
Example: With a AED 1.00 bid, we may lower it for low-probability clicks, but we will never exceed AED 1.00.
Fixed
Set your default bid amount. You'll bid that specific value for every click without any real-time adjustments.
Example: With a AED 1.00 bid, you will bid exactly AED 1.00 for every auction, regardless of conversion likelihood.

Negative Keyword Targeting

Phrase Match: If you enter "red shirt", negative phrase match will not show your ad on any search query that has the phrase "red shirt".

Exact Match: If you enter "red shirt", negative exact match will not show your ad only on the exact "red shirt" search query.

Exclude specific keywords to prevent your ads from appearing in relevant searches. You can add up to 20 Exact and 20 Phrase negative targets.

7. Top of Search Placement Bidding (Optional)
i
Bid for Top of Search Placement

Top of Search Bidding allows you to increase your bid specifically when your ad is eligible to appear in the top position of search results.

You can set a bid adjustment between 10% and 900%. When your ad qualifies for the top slot, your base bid will be increased by the selected percentage.

Example: If your base bid is AED 1.00 and you set a top of slot adjustment of 50%, your bid will increase to AED 1.50 when aiming for the top slot.

Boost your base bid by up to 900% to compete for top of search placements.
